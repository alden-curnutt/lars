Stable Families Make Stable Communities

At LARS, we promote stability in the lives of community members in many ways. Check out our services below.

LARS would not be able to help individuals and families in need without the generous support of its partners, which include its volunteers, individual donors, local businesses, local congregations, Laurel City, local counties, United Way, and foundations. We appreciate all donations and grants to LARS, whether big or small.

LARS assists those who need and want to achieve long-term self-sufficiency through tailored services, including:

  • Long-term case management
  • Employment support
  • Referrals to services
  • Transitional housing program
  • Permanent housing program
