LARS Holiday Program

This past holiday season, generous support from local families, community groups, and congregations allowed us to fulfill Christmas wish lists for 426 children and distribute loose gifts to an additional 117 children, along with holiday meals for each family. A huge thanks to all those who provided for these families and volunteered their time to help brighten their holidays!
