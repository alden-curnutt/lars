LARS Holiday Program

During the 2015 holiday season, generous support from local families, community groups, and congregations allowed us to fulfill Christmas wish lists for 426 children and distribute loose gifts to an additional 117 children, along with holiday meals for each family.

Families will begin making their wish lists in early October for the 2016 holiday season. Donors and volunteers interested in contributing to this year's Holiday Program can contact Alli Milner at 301-776-0442 ext. 34, or check back around mid-September to submit your information through this web page.
