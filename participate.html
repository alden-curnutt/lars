Participate!

LARS hosts two 5K races each year: the Spring into Summer 5K in May and a larger Turkey Trot 5K on Thanksgiving Morning. This year, LARS is proud to host our 4th Annual Spring into Summer 5K and 1 Mile Walk! The race will take place at McCullough Field on May 5th, 2018 at 8AM and will follow the same route (can we highlight “the same route here” with the route link as well?) as LARS’ past 5Ks. We are excited to share this fun day with our many LARS supporters, local businesses, volunteers, and members of the community. Sign up here to support this amazing event!
