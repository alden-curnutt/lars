Participate!

LARS hosts two 5K races each year: the Spring into Summer 5K in Spring and a larger Turky Trot 5K in November.

For this Spring 2017 season, LARS is proud to be partnering with First Generation College Bound for a 5K to kick off Laurel High School’s Community Day. The 5K run and 1 mile walk will take place at McCullough Field, starting at 8AM and following the same route as LARS’ past 5Ks. We are excited to be sharing this fun day with FGCB, a great local organization making college access and success a reality for low-income students in Laurel.
