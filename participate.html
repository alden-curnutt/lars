Participate!

LARS hosts a popular Turkey Trot 5K run and 1 mile walk on Thanksgiving morning. A smaller 5K and 1 mile walk is held at the end of May. Both events are held at McCullough Field in Laurel and run the same USATF certified course throughout scenic Historic Laurel. All of the proceeds from these events benefit LARS' work to end hunger and homelessness in Laurel. Save the dates for 2016/2017:

12th Annual 5K Turkey Trot - November 24, 2016

3rd Annual Spring into Summer 5K - May 20, 2017
