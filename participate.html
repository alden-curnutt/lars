Spring into Summer

Saturday, June 20th, 2015 at McCullough Field (8th Street, Laurel MD)

To celebrate the first day of summer and Father's Day weekend, LARS is planning a brand new 5K & 1 Mile Walk to benefit our efforts to end hunger and homelessness in Laurel. Enjoy great food, music, and post-race fun after challenging yourself to a 5K run throughout Old Towne Laurel or a 1 mile walk around the field!
