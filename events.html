Get Involved With LARS

LARS Sponsors several events throughout the year including the Seasonal 5k community races and Holiday Program. Check out or pages for updates or new events. LARS is always looking for new and effective ways to make the community a stronger, better palce to be.

Spring into Summer

Saturday, June 20th, 2015 at McCullough Field (8th Street, Laurel MD)

To celebrate the first day of summer and Father's Day weekend, LARS is planning a brand new 5K & 1 Mile Walk to benefit our efforts to end hunger and homelessness in Laurel. Enjoy great food, music, and post-race fun after challenging yourself to a 5K run throughout Old Towne Laurel or a 1 mile walk around the field!

Turkey Trot

One of Laurel's biggest events, the Turkey Trot is a 5k and 1 mile walk to benefit the Laurel communities.

While the event occurs every Thanksgiving season, you can still prepare while the temperatures are still warm!
