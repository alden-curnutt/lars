Many Ways to Donate

  • Send a Check. Please make checks out to "Laurel Advocacy & Referral Services, Inc". Checks can be mailed to: 311 Laurel Avenue, Laurel, MD 20707.
  • Donate Online. Clicking on the Give2Charity link on this page will take you to the LARS online donation page provided by Local Independent Charities of America. Visa and MasterCard donations are processed free of charge. American Express donations are assessed a low transaction fee of 2.85%.
  • Donate a Vehicle. The Vehicle Donation Program is a free service for converting that extra car, truck, or RV into a tax-deductible donation benefiting LARS.
  • Give at Work. Federal employees and those with a United Way workplace giving program can designate contributions through their respective Combined Federal Campaign (CFC) or United Way campaign. Our United Way number is 8843. Our CFC number is 92544.

LARS is a private non-profit charity recognized by the Internal Revenue Service as a 501(c)3 organization. We are incorporated in the State of Maryland. Contributions to LARS are tax deductible to the fullest extent of the law.
