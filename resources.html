Homeless Resources

First Call for Help (Homeless Shelter Hotline):

Dial 2-1-1 from a landline or 1-888-731-0999. The Homeless Hotline provides emergency shelter referrals 24 hours a day, 365 days a year for homeless individuals and families in the state of Maryland.
