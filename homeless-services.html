LARS Homeless Services

If you are seeking immediate shelter, please dial 2-1-1 or visit 2-1-1.org to find the direct number for your county. 2-1-1 provides free and confidential information and referral, including help with food, housing, employment, health care, counseling and more.

LARS has limited funds to assist homeless clients with the following:

  • Obtaining identification cards
  • Obtaining birth certificates
  • Obtaining prescriptions and other minor health expenses
  • First month's rent or security deposit
  • Obtaining drug treatment and certain mental health needs
  • Financial support for clients to get the needed materials to start work, and assistance with child care expenses

LARS Provides Support Services & Outreach

LARS provides support services and outreach to homeless individuals and families under a grant from the United States Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD). Some of the support services provided include case management, employment assistance, first month's rent, transportation, and birth certificate fees. LARS also conducts intake assessments for winter shelter participants on behalf of local congregations.
