The LARS Employment Center

The LARS Employment Center is open to community members over the age of 18 who reside in the Greater Laurel area. A job coach is available to meet with job seekers ONLY on Fridays from 9am – 12pm. Clients will be seen on a first-come, first-served basis.

Please come prepared with an updated resume (if available) and information including:

  • Your previous employment
  • Name and address of former employer(s)
  • Dates you were employed
  • Name, address and phone number of a person who can serve as a reference from the job

This information enable the LARS staff to better help you with employment opportunities. For questions about the LARS Employment Program, contact Janada Randall at 301-776-0442 ext. 37.
