The LARS Employment Center & Computer Lab

Employment Program Information

The LARS Employment Center and Computer Lab is open to community members over the age of 18 who reside in the Greater Laurel area. A job coach is available to meet with job seekers ONLY on Fridays from 9am – 12pm. Clients will be seen on a first-come, first-served basis.

Please come prepared with an updated resume (if you have one) and information regarding your previous employment, including: name and address of former employer(s), dates you were employed, and the name, address and phone number of a person who can serve as a reference from the job. This information will help you be more productive with your time in the computer lab.

For questions about the LARS Employment Program, contact Janada Randall at 301-776-0442 ext. 37.
