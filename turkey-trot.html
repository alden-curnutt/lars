LARS • Turkey Trot

This Thanksgiving Morning, over 800 runners, walkers, and spectators will flock to Laurel’s Historic District for the 12th Annual 5K Turkey Trot to Benefit Laurel Advocacy & Referral Services, Inc. (LARS).

This event has gained a loyal following and continues to grow each year, raising crucial funds for LARS, a non-profit organization helping homeless and low-income families and individuals achieve stability and self-sufficiency. Thanks to the support of many local businesses and organizations who helped us cover our event expenses, the net proceeds from last year’s event exceeded $45,000! This money is used to provide food, financial help, and housing to Laurel residents in crisis.
